Burroughs 70s Art Rock

The ethos behind Wolcott’s Instant Pain Annihilator begins with the name. Smith exposes our ailments, and sets them free into a world of dark power-pop, with moments that are exciting and surreal with fantastical synths, and others that are raw and real, and leave you feeling like a Burroughs-esq cut-up.

Drawing inspirations from Kevin Ayers, Genesis’ The Lamb Lies Down On Broadway, Talk Talk, and Magazine, Wolcott’s Instant Pain Annihilator aspires to adventure further into fantasy and question our own reality.
